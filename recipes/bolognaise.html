Spaghetti Bolognaise

Ingredients

  • 15g Flora Buttery
  • 1 carrot, very finely chopped
  • 1 onion
  • 1 celery stick, optional, chopped
  • 55g mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • 350g lean beef mince
  • ground black pepper
  • 400g tinned tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • 3 tbsp beef stock
  • 225g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese

Recipe Steps

  1. Heat the Flora and sauté the onion, carrot and celery for 10 minutes without browning. Add the mushrooms and mince, and cook until evenly brown. Add remaining ingredients except spaghetti and cheese and bring to the boil, stirring continuously. Cover and simmer gently for 1–1½ hours.

  2. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti in lightly salted boiling water for 12–15 minutes, then drain.

  3. Arrange spaghetti in a deep dish, pour meat sauce over and sprinkle with grated cheese.
